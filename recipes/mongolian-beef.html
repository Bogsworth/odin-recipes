"Mongolian" Beef Stir Fry

It's a delicious, but probably not nutricious meal!

It's almost quick enough for a weeknight meal

Ingredients

  • Brocoli
  • Flank Steak
  • Garlic
  • Green Onions
  • Fresh Ginger, Grated
  • Brown Sugar
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Corn Starch
  • Soy Sauce

Directions

  1. Chop steak and combine with corn starch
  2. Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, garlic, ginger, 2 tsp of vegetable oil, and water. Heat until slightly thickened (5-10 mins)
  3. heat vegetable oil in large pan. Add beef and fry until browned and cooked through. Transfer to paper towel lined plate
  4. Add sauce mixture to saucepan and combine with beef. Cook until sauce thickens
  5. Top with green onions, and serve!
