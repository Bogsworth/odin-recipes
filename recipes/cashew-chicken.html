Cashew Chicken Stir Fry

A basic but delicious stir fry!

It's probably even reasonably healthy.

...Depending on how much honey you add...

Ingredients

  • 3 tbsp cornstarch
  • 0.5 tsp salt
  • 0.5 tsp pepper
  • 1.25 lb of boneless, skinless chicken breasts, diced into 1-inch pieces
  • 2 tbsp seasame oil
  • 1 tblsn olive oil
  • 2 cups broccoli florets
  • 1 cup red bell peppers, diced
  • 1 cup shelled, frozen edamame
  • 1 cup unsalted, dry-roasted whole cashews
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbpn honey, or to taste
  • 1 tbsp rice wine vinegar
  • 1 tbpn chili garlic sauce
  • 3/4 tsp ground ginger
  • 1 cup green onions, sliced into thin rounds

Directions

  1. Add cornstarch, chicken, salt peper to ziplog back, and shake
  2. To a medium bowl add the soy sauce, honey, rice wine vinegar, chili-garlic sauce, and ginger. Whisk to combine
  3. To large skillet add the oils and chicken. Cook for ~5 minutes over medium high heat until 90% cooked through
  4. Add the broccoli, peppers, edemame, and garlic. Stir to combine. Cook until veggies are crisp and chicken is cooked through
  5. Add cashews to the skillet and combine
  6. Ad the sauce and stir to combine. Allow sauce to simmer over medium low heat for 1-2 minutes.
  7. Add the green onions, stir to combine and serve over rice!
