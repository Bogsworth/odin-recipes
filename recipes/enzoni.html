Enzoni Cocktail

How to create an Enzoni cocktail.

It's a riff on a Negroni, but with gin and grapes.

It's a highly refreshing summer drink!

Ingredients

  • 1 oz Gin
  • 1 oz Campari
  • 0.75 oz lemon juice
  • .5 oz simple syrup, rich
  • 5 green grapes

Directions

  1. Muddle the grapes in your shaker
  2. Add the remaining ingredients
  3. Shake with ice (one large cube, one broken)
  4. Strain over fresh ice into a double old fashioned glass
  5. Garnish with skewered grapes
